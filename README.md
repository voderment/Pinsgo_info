# Pinsgo

Pinsgo is a lightweight macOS translation app designed for fast desktop workflows.

It helps you translate content without breaking focus, whether you are reading, writing, researching, or working across languages.

## What Pinsgo does

- Translate selected text from other apps
- Translate typed or pasted text quickly
- Capture part of your screen and extract text with OCR
- Launch translation tools from the menu bar
- Use built-in translation features or supported cloud translation services
- Customize hotkeys and workflow settings for daily use

## Who it is for

Pinsgo is built for people who work with multiple languages on Mac, including:

- students and researchers
- writers and knowledge workers
- developers and product teams
- multilingual users who need quick translation during everyday work

## Product philosophy

Translation should feel like part of your desktop workflow, not a separate task.

That is the idea behind Pinsgo: quick access, lightweight interaction, and practical tools that stay out of your way.
