# Pinsgo Privacy Policy

date: 2025-06-01

## Overview
Pinsgo is a macOS desktop application that provides AI-powered translation. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

## Data We Process
- **User input text**: Sent to the selected translation provider to generate results. We do not store input or output text on our servers.
- **Configuration data**: API keys, model IDs, and endpoints that you configure locally. The app stores secrets inside the macOS keychain.
- **Usage diagnostics**: Pinsgo does not collect analytics or crash reports. Any such data remains on your device unless you share it manually.

## Third-Party Services
Pinsgo can call third-party translation APIs (e.g., Apple Translation, Volcengine Ark/Doubao, SiliconFlow). When you enable these services, your text and credentials are subject to each provider's own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing those policies before using the service.

## Security Practices
- Secrets are stored using the macOS Keychain and never transmitted elsewhere except to the selected translation provider.
- Network requests are made directly from your device to the provider endpoints you configure.
- We do not operate cloud servers for Pinsgo; all logic runs locally on your Mac.

## Your Choices
- You may remove or rotate API keys at any time within the app's settings.
- You can disable third-party providers or use the built-in Apple Translation engine if you prefer to avoid external services.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact: support@pinsgo.app
